What is Mindfulness?

Mindfulness is the practice of purposely bringing one's attention to the present-moment experience without evaluation, a skill one develops through meditation or other training..

Why is this necessary?

help people manage stress, cope better with serious illness and reduce anxiety and depression.

who practice mindfulness report an increased ability to relax, a greater enthusiasm for life and improved self-esteem.
